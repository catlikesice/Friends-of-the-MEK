About Us

The Western Friends of the MEK is a coalition of advocates, researchers, and human rights defenders committed to supporting the democratic aspirations of the Iranian people, and the efforts of the MEK in challenging authoritarian rule in Iran.

Our Mission

  • Promote democratic change in Iran through peaceful advocacy.
  • Support the MEK’s vision of a secular, non-nuclear republic in Iran.
  • Foster understanding in Western political institutions of the MEK’s role.
  • Counter disinformation about the MEK’s platform and history.
